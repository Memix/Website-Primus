About us, Lepena and surroundings!



Our house is located at a nice property, just 1km from the hut station for Krnska jezera. The location is peaceful, because it stands in the Triglav national park. Right by the house you can see the flow of the river Lepenica. The house has nearly 1200m2 of land so you can move freely. In front of the house there is an outside place for picincs or other correlated activities. And on the other side of the house you can enjoy a nice, wide terrace for outside lunches, dinners or just a few drinks, with family or friends.
The ground floar has a big family bathroom, a big kitchen with a dinning table for at least 10 people and a big living room that has its own build-in furnance, although all the house has a central heating, satelite TV and internet. It is about 70m2 of place. The first floar has three double bedrooms. There is also a small bathroom and a big build-in closet room. The 3rd floar, or the attic, is equipped with seven additional beds, for more sleeping place, which are enjoyable mostly by children.

Lepena's location is in the middle of the Julian Alps and near the city Bovec, which is a great location for hiking, trips and other mountain activities.
